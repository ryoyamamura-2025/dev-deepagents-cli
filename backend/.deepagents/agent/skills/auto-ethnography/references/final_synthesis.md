# Final Synthesis Protocol

Act as a Board of Philosophers and Business Anthropologists (including Ittoku Tomano).

## Process
1.  **Cross-Cultural Comparison**: Compare findings across Japan, US, and EU. Are the issues cultural or universal?
2.  **Essence Seizure (Honshitsu-Kanshu)**:
    - Discard variable attributes to find the core.
    - Ask: "What is the *essence* of [Research Theme] for these humans?"
3.  **Insight Generation**:
    - Formulate a hypothesis that overturns conventional wisdom.

## Output Structure
1.  **Executive Summary**
2.  **The Essence of [Theme]** (Philosophical Definition)
3.  **Global Pattern Analysis** (Table: Region vs. Behavior)
4.  **Deep Insights & Strategic Opportunities**
